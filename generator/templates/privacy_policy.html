Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 7, 2025

Post Genius (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered social media automation platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • User Account Data: When you sign up, we collect your name, email, and password (securely hashed).
  • Profile Data: You may optionally upload a profile image and write a bio.
  • OAuth Access Tokens: When you connect Facebook or Instagram, we securely store your access tokens to post content on your behalf.
  • Generated Content: We store captions and image prompts that you generate via GPT-4 and Stable Diffusion APIs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To allow AI-based generation of social media content
  • To post content on your connected Facebook and Instagram accounts (with your consent)
  • To analyze and export your content history
  • To personalize your experience and dashboard

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your tokens and content are only used within the application to serve your intended actions.

4. Data Security

All access tokens and user information are stored securely. Tokens are encrypted and never exposed in plaintext. Access is restricted to authorized application components only.

5. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Used to generate captions via GPT-4
  • Replicate: Used to generate images via Stable Diffusion
  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): OAuth and Graph API access for publishing content

6. Your Rights

You may update your profile, delete your account, or disconnect your social accounts at any time. To remove your data, please contact us at the email below.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests related to your data, email us at: support@postgenius.ai
